WARRIORS BAND

Loud is good, fast is better, loud and fast is best!

We are the official pep band for University of Waterloo! Our job is to attend sports games and hype up the crowd with our music. Even though the band was created for attending sports games we attend other events on campus and around the city!

REQUIREMENTS

Pay the membership fee of $10 and attend 2 rehersals per term. The membership fee covers both the main band and drumline. No instrument or experience is required!

DRUMLINE

Taking inspiration from organizations such as DCI and WGI the Warriors Band Drumline plays battery percussion pieces at sporting events and other events around campus and the city. The Warriors Band Drumline is open to people of all experience whether you have played percussion for years or have never touched a drum, come join us at one of our two rehearsals every week to play some drums!

REQUIREMENTS

Pay the membership fee of $10 and print off your music. The membership fee covers both the main band and drumline. No instrument is required but bringing your own sticks is encouraged.
